FreaKing

WE ARE FREAKS!

FreaKing is a Circus where you can be one of the performers called the "Freaks". The FreaKing recruits people who have unique, uncommon, and unsusual features about their appearances and give them the chance to showcase any talent they have and to perform in the circus.

The FreaKing currently has a dwarf performer, a super tall human being, a woman that has beard, a woman with extraordinary strength, talented twins and many more. These lovely people are most commonly not accepted in the society and the jobs outside but FreaKing is a great opportunity for them to earn and be happy at the same time!
